I love movies and filmmaking. I love making my own films and watching films.

Start

Ever since I was little, movies have been a huge part of my life. I have always loved going to the movie theater. I’ve always been drawn to the art of filmmaking, and the excitement I feel when watching a great film has stayed with me throughout the years. I got a camera for Christmas one year and have made my own short films and things like that ever since.

Goals and Inspirations

I hope to create a good enough short film to submit to a festival. My ultimate goal is to become a movie director, and I’m determined to work hard and learn as much as I can along the way. Some filmmakers who inspire me are Steven Spielberg, Tim Burton, and Jim Henson (to name a few).
